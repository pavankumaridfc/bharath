Vision & Mission

Our Vision :

  • To enable innovations in healthcare and environment for a better tomorrow and a better treatment opportunity.
  • To create an ecosystem in the campus which offers every student a chance to be innovative, creative and tactical to pursue his or her dreams and gain economic, technical and entrepreneurial expertise in their chosen field, thus realizing the vision of the Sree Balaji Medical College and Hospital (SBMCH), Bharath Institute of Higher Education (BIHER).
  • To make every man a success and no man a failure.

Our Mission :

  • To create an ecosystem that will enable innovation and knowledge-based entrepreneurship to improve quality of life.
  • To promote sustainable development while taking the innovations from lab to land.
  • To encourage the concept of techno-entrepreneurship, enable creation and sustainability of viable Bio & Healthcare enterprises.

Summary of the Project

Turmeric-incorporated nanocomposite band-aids has good medicinal values with anti-microbial and fungal activities, which can be used for healing various wounds without any harmful side effects. Turmeric contains more than 300 naturally occurring components, it accelerates fast healing of wounds and it stimulates growth of new tissues by stimulating ant-inflammatory activities. These attractive properties of turmeric have highly motivated and encouraged to invent a cost-effective and efficient Band-Aid to treat complicated wounds. But the great anti-inflammatory powers of turmeric could not be fully utilized so far for lack of a reliable application system. The nanoparticulate curcumin (molecule of turmeric) formulation is prepared along with the biodegradable polymer that can be absorbed in the wound site itself.
